Food Access Locator

Welcome to the Food Access Locator for Dane County! The goal of this webmap is to help connect people with resources. You can use this map to find food services, learn where to donate food, and to find potential partners to collaborate with to make food accessible for everyone!

Help
  • The purpose of this map is to help you find resources. Whether you are an idividual who needs to find food for yourself; an individual, group, or company that would like to donate food; or part of an organization that would like to work with other organizations to distribute food; this map is for you. To use this map, open the "Services" and "Providers" tabs by clicking on them. Click on the box(es) that best represent what you are looking for.
